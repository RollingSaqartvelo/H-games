Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

H-GAMES Provider ("we", "us") operates the Outlaw Escape game as a Telegram Mini App. This page explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

  • Telegram user data — your Telegram user ID, first name, last name, and username, provided automatically by Telegram when you launch the Mini App.
  • Game activity — bets placed, cashout multipliers, win/loss history, and session timestamps.
  • Balance — your in-game balance used for placing bets.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To identify your account and maintain your in-game balance.
  • To record game history for provably fair verification.
  • To display your profile within the app.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored on our secure server. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Game results are stored to support provably fair auditing.

4. Telegram Platform

This app runs inside Telegram. By using it you also agree to Telegram's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion by contacting support.

6. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us via Telegram.
